Itahari International College

Since 2017, Itahari International College has offered top-tier IT and Business programmes in partnership with London Metropolitan University, UK. It aims to produce globally competitive graduates, making it the first college in Itahari to provide a direct UK university degree. IIC nurtures future leaders and innovators by providing hands-on experiences, real-world projects, and a practical curriculum. Our commitment is evident in our alumni's successful careers and high placement rates. Itahari International College is a flagship institution of Innovate Nepal Group (ING). London Metropolitan University is a diverse and socially inclusive institution in London, UK. With over 12,500 UK-based students and collaborative programmes worldwide, London Met provides education focusing on sustainability and employability.
